Sage the AI in Sonic 3 AIR

An upcoming Sonic 3 AIR mod by TimerBunneh & LegionTheSpiritomb.

Sage the AI in Sonic 3 AIR aims to merge the present with the past by making Sage, from Sonic Frontiers, into a playable character.

Current Features

  • Custom Sonic 3-styled sprites
  • A soundtrack that can be tailored to one's liking
  • An original super/hyper form
  • Achievements exclusive to the mod, courtesy of Achievements Plus
  • And more...?
This mod is currently in early development and will not be released for a while. For updates, please keep an eye on the dedicated Twitter thread, which can be read from the beginning here.
